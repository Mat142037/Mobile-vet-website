Bringing professional and affordable care to your door

At FetchCare Mobile Vets, we bring professional veterinary care right to your home where your pets feel most comfortable. Whether it's a routine check up, vaccination, diagnostics or end of life care, our fully equipped mobile clinic ensures your pet gets the attention they need without the stress of a traditional vet visit. Serving the Rochdale area, we're dedicated to making veterinary care more accessible, personalized, and stress free for both pets and their people.
