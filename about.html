About Us

At Fetchcare Mobile Vets, we believe quality veterinary care should be as stress‐free and convenient as possible for both pets and their families. That's why we bring compassionate, full-service veterinary care directly to your doorstep. Our team of experienced veterinarians and technicians are passionate animal lovers dedicated to keeping your pets healthy, comfortable, and happy. From routine checkups and vaccinations to wellness exams, diagnostics, and senior pet care, we provide personalized services tailored to meet each pet's unique needs all in the familiar comfort of home.
By eliminating the stress of car rides, waiting rooms, and unfamiliar environments, we create a calmer, safer experience for your pet and peace of mind for you. With our mobile clinic, we're not just making veterinary care more accessible, we're redefining what it means to care for pets and the people who love them. Because at Fetchcare Mobile Vets, your pet's health is our mission, and their comfort is our promise.

We purchased our mobile vehicle 2025 and after a few modifications she was ready to hit the road. Everybody meet Wendy! Our clinic-on-wheels isn't just any ride it's a fully equipped veterinary hospital that just happens to come with four wheels and a whole lot of heart. Think of it as the "pet care express," rolling right up to your driveway with everything we need to keep tails wagging and whiskers twitching.
Inside, you'll find a state-of-the-art exam space, cozy comforts for nervous pets, and all the tools we need for checkups, vaccinations, diagnostics, and more. From the outside, you might notice excited pets peeking out their windows as we pull up because they know the vet is coming to them!
So when you see our vehicle roll through your neighborhood, give us a wave we're probably on our way to deliver smiles, snuggles, and healthier tomorrows.
